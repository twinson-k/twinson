Ground Water Contamination and Its Impacts

Groundwater contamination can occur from various sources, including industrial spills, agricultural runoff, and improper waste disposal. Contaminants such as heavy metals, pesticides, and organic chemicals can compromise water quality and pose serious health risks. Addressing contamination involves rigorous monitoring, remediation strategies, and regulatory measures to protect public health and the environment.
